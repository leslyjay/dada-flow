Our Story

Dada Flow was born from the belief that every girl, no matter her background, deserves dignity and access to education. In communities where period poverty silences dreams and limits movement, we rise — offering kits, mentorship, and safe spaces. We are not just giving products; we are building confidence and rewriting futures.

Meet the Founder

As a girl who once walked the same dusty paths with unanswered questions and unmet needs, our founder chose to become the solution. What started as a whisper of change became a movement — reaching schools, villages, and hearts. Her vision is bold: no girl left behind. Through her leadership, Dada Flow has grown from a one-woman mission to a national initiative impacting thousands.
