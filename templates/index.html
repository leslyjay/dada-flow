Empowering Girls. Transforming Communities.

Dada Flow provides dignity kits, mentorship, and education for girls in underserved communities.

About Dada Flow

We are a youth-led initiative driving period dignity, reproductive health education, and empowerment across Kenya. Through community engagement and advocacy, we break stigma and create real change.

Our Impact

Over 5,000 girls reached in 12 counties. 300+ mentorship sessions delivered. We aim to expand across East Africa with strategic partners and funders.
